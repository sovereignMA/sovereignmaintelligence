FAQ

How does the 21-day free trial work?

Sign up and get full access to all 21 agents, your deal pipeline, intelligence scraper, and vault — no credit card required. After 21 days you'll be prompted to choose a plan. Your data is never deleted.

Can I refer other dealmakers?

Yes. Every account has a unique referral link in the nav menu. When someone you refer subscribes, you both receive 30 days free added to your account automatically.

How does No Money Down work?

80% deferred consideration paid from business cashflow over 3 years + 20% ABL secured against receivables. The business acquires itself.

What does Zero Personal Guarantees mean?

All debt sits at SPV level. Your home and personal assets are never at risk.

No. Sovereign is an unregulated M&A introductory service. Not FCA-authorised.
