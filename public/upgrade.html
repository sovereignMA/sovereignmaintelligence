Questions
What happens to my data when the trial ends? +
Nothing is deleted. Your pipeline, deal notes, and target list are all preserved. You enter read-only mode — you can see everything but can't take new actions. Upgrade at any point to restore full access instantly.
Do I need a credit card for the trial? +
No card required for Solo and Deal Team trials. Fund/PE tier requires a card upfront given the white-glove onboarding involved.
Can I import my existing target spreadsheet? +
Yes. CSV import is available on all plans. Deal Team and above get column-mapping support and bulk enrichment. If you want it done for you, the Done-for-You Setup add-on covers this.
How does the referral programme work? +
Share your referral link from the "Invite & Earn" button in your profile menu. When someone you refer subscribes to a paid plan, you both receive 30 days of free access added to your next billing cycle.
Is annual billing worth it? +
Annual saves you 2 months — the equivalent of roughly £300–£2,400 depending on plan. We recommend monthly to start, then switching to annual once you've validated the tool in your deal process (typically 30–60 days in).
Can I get a community or academy discount? +
Yes — if you're a member of Dealmakers Academy, Unity Group, Dealmaker Wealth, or Acquisition Lab, reach out for a community rate. We partner with several academies to offer co-branded plans for their members.
