1. Purpose and Scope

This Information Asset Register ("Register") identifies and classifies all information assets associated with Project Sovereign, in accordance with ISO 27001:2022 Annex A 5.9 (Inventory of information and other associated assets).

The Register covers all assets that:

  • Store, process, or transmit Platform data
  • Are required for delivery of Platform services
  • Are operated by Project Sovereign or by third-party providers on its behalf

Assets are reviewed and updated annually, or when a material change occurs (new service onboarded, decommissioning, breach affecting an asset).

8. Review and Maintenance

This Register is maintained by the ISMS Owner (Howard Henry) and reviewed:

  • Annually (next review: 4 April 2027)
  • When a new third-party service is onboarded (within 5 business days)
  • When a service is decommissioned (within 2 business days)
  • Following any security incident affecting an asset

Asset decommissioning must include: credential revocation, data deletion confirmation from provider, removal from this Register, and a compliance_log entry with event type asset_decommission.
